Lux Driver Assistant — Your Profitability Co-Pilot

Do you know how much you really earn per minute on each ride? Lux Driver Assistant automatically calculates your profitability in real time, right on your screen, while you drive.

HOW IT WORKS

The app reads ride information displayed by Uber and 99 (fare, time, and distance) and instantly calculates:

- Real net profit (after deducting fuel, maintenance, and depreciation)
- Profitability in BRL/minute
- Visual traffic light: Green = accept / Yellow = caution / Red = decline

FEATURES

SMART OVERLAY
- Floating card appears automatically when a ride is offered
- Shows net value prominently (large font, easy to read while driving)
- Colors: green (good ride), yellow (caution), red (not worth it)

CUSTOMIZABLE TRAFFIC LIGHT
- Set your own profitability thresholds
- Dual slider to adjust minimum and ideal values
- Adapts to your cost of living and vehicle type

REAL COST CALCULATION
- Configure: vehicle fuel consumption, fuel price
- Configure: maintenance cost per km
- Configure: vehicle depreciation
- Result: how much you REALLY take home

SIMPLE SETUP
- Guided onboarding in 3 steps
- Runs in the background without slowing your phone
- Compatible with Uber and 99

PRIVACY AND SECURITY

- No personal data is collected or transmitted
- Works 100% offline
- Does not interfere with Uber or 99 operation
- Uses Accessibility Service only to read values displayed on screen

REQUIREMENTS

- Android 7.0 or higher
- Accessibility Permission (to read the ride card)
- Overlay Permission (to display the floating card)

Stop accepting rides in the dark. With Lux, every decision is based on real data.

Drive smarter. Earn more.
